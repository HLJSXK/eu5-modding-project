### Mod changes: ###
# Prosperity decay added to each age. Unlike every other new value, the decay reduces with each age.
# This represents the world become a bit more prosperous over time.

# From age 2 onwards: buildings & rgos become more expensive (up to 100% more expensive by the last age).
# This is a direct anti snowballing measure. However, prices should still feel reasonable and not prohibitive.

# Food consumption also increase up to +50% in the last age. As society develops and food abundancy increases,
# it is only natural that people will eat more on average. 
# There is no increase in the first age, otherwise the AI would struggle too much and likely starve for a while.

# City & town upgrades become more expensive with each age (up to +50% by the last age)

# Roads are also made more expensive, but not via age bonuses. Refer to in_game/common/prices/AS_00_hardcoded.txt
# AS mod is too strict on this, I have lower the scaling a bit
