## Little Ice Age Mitigation (lia) — country and location scope auto_modifiers.
# Controlled by CMM toggle sol__lia (default ON).
# When ON: applies SOL relief offsets on top of vanilla LIA modifiers (net effect = milder LIA).
# When OFF: vanilla LIA modifiers apply at full harsh strength.
#
# Province-scope LIA modifiers (short_harvest_modifier, recent_white_storm_modifier) are NOT
# covered here — province auto_modifiers are not supported by EU5. Those remain as permanent
# TRY_REPLACE in A_SOL_province.txt.
