####### Version 1.2.0.0
#############################################################

#############################################################
####### General
#############################################################

- Adjusted files to work with the beta patch
- Reworked the general Idea GUI to hopefully fix most issues with different resolutions
- Added two new tabs in the Idea Window:
- 1) Tab for National Ideas (not implemented yet and therefore deactivated)
- 2) Tab for Idea Statistics (Get an overview over all Ideagroups and their bonuses)
- Reworked the Ideagroup Window
- Changed the way of how Researcher Cost is being calculated. Now way less at the start, however if your economy grows its gonna be big
The new formula is: Researcher Upkeep is calculated as follows:\n( ( Number of researched Advances * Dominant Ability of Researcher) + Economical Base of [Player.GetNameWithNoTooltip] ) * ( Number of Researched Advances * 0.05 ) * 0.00007 * Upkeep Spending Percentage
- Fixed: Researchers that were not hired via the generic action staying active after dying
- Fixed: You can no longer see the skills of researchers to soft reload them before hiring
- Guard and Marine Troop Types now show what unlocks them in the techtree
- Split the Ideagroup Available Alert in two: Unlock new IG Slot and New IG can be selected
- Fixed: Occurences when alerts should be hidden but were visible
- Reduced base Innovation growth for the player to 1 (2 for the AI)
- Innovation Growth from Researchers has been increased slightly
- General Idea Cost has been increased from 300 to 400
- Fixed: Metropolis Upgrade not costing anything (now the base cost is 2500 Gold)

####### Idea Variation 2 Version 1.0 (Original Release Version)
################################################################################
################################################################################


#############################################################
####### Main Features
#############################################################

- Reintroduced EU IVs Idea System to EU V
- 4 categories of Ideagroups: Admin, Diplomatic, Army and Navy
- Fully Customizable Number of Ideagroup Slots and Idea Cost
- Unlock Ideagroups and new Idearoup Slots by spending Innovation points
- At least 15 generic different Ideagroups per category
- Over 30 special religious Ideagroups 
- Unique Government Type and Government Rank Ideagroups
- Automatic changing of Religious, Government Type/Rank Ideagroups
- Hire Researchers to boost your generation of Innovation Points
- Unique Ideabonuses like new Casus Belli, new Buildings and new Modifiers
- New Units: Guard Units and Marines
